MANKATHA

Mankatha is a Tamil action horror film released on 31 August 2011This movie is directed by Venkat Prabhu . The film stars Ajith Kumar , Trisha , Arjun , Lakshmi Roy , Anjali and Andrea Jeremiah in lead roles. Dayanidhi Alagiri is producing with music composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja and cinematography by Sakthi Saravanan. The film is Ajith Kumar's 50th film.The movie is produced by Dayanidhi Alagiri's Cloud Nayan Movies Kalaiyakam .
